# Vibe Player Bot

**UNDER CONSTRUCTION**

A "Background music" discord bot for your server to chill together.

## Background

People enjoyed istening to background music like instrumental song and Lo-Fi when they're studying or talking with friends. Sometimes the problem is finding the exact music or song you want as a background music playlist on youtube, or changing the mood by adding and changing white noise. With this bot, you can do that together! Whether you're on a group study or just chatting in general, let this bot accompany your voice channel to let the vibe in. 

## Features

- Play music from several sources with help of `yt-dlp` and other direct API parser (optional as this requires you to get your own API key).
- Add, delete or shuffle playlist on the fly.
- Dynamically switch white noise or change its volume.
- Manage playlists to create different mood or music genre.
